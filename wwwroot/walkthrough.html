Level One

Escape from the Cage

Show
  1. Click the bin to remove the lid
  2. Click the paperclip to pick it up
  3. Click the house door to start the picklock minigame
  4. Click the direction buttons to pick the lock, the correct order is:
    Up, Up, Left, Right, Up, Down, Left, Down, Right, Left
  5. Click on the key in the house, and then click on the cage padlock to unlock it
  6. Click the rope hanging on the tree and Paddy will use a bamboo stick to hook it
  7. Click on the stake in the ground, then click on the roof of the house to put the stake in the roof
  8. Click Paddy and he will throw the rope over the stake
  9. Click Paddy to move down the rope
  10. Click Paddy to jump off the roof
  11. Click the house windows to open them
  12. Click Paddy to end the level

Level Two

Silent but Sneaky!

Show
  1. Click the painting on the wall
  2. Click the drill to pick it up
  3. Click the screws in the following order to open the trapdoor
    Bottom left, top right, top left, bottom right
  4. Click on the net, and then the trapdoor to drop the net down the hole
  5. Click the bed sheet and then the trapdoor to cover the trapdoor
  6. Click the alarm clock, and then click the hands until the time says 4.15
  7. Click ‘Set Alarm’ and the alarm will go off
  8. Once the guard has fallen down the trap click Paddy to end the level
Hide

Level Three

Give a Bird a Home

Show
  1. Click the Bushes, then the straw bundle that appears to pick it up.
  2. Click the lower tree branch on the rightmost tree to start building a nest
  3. Click the rock pile to reveal more straw, again put this on the nest
  4. Click on the hole in the leftmost tree to reveal the bird with straw in its beak, again put this onto the nest
  5. Click the eggs to swap them around, the order should be
    Green, Orange, Blue, Purple, Pink, Yellow
  6. Click the mother bird and she will fly up to her nest
  7. Click Paddy to end the level
Hide

Level Four

A Steep Ascent

Show
  1. Click the cog which is hidden behind the bush
  2. Click the lever switch to activate the pulley
  3. Click Paddy and he will climb onto the platform
  4. Click the 3rd apple in the tree, once it is on the floor click it again and a worm will pop out
  5. Click the worm, then the woodpecker in the tree, the woodpecker will peck the tree causing a rock to fall out
  6. Click this rock, then click the left side platform of the pulley, the weight will cause the pulley to move a bit
  7. Click the shovel leaning against the cave, then dig along the upper level to reveal two more rocks
  8. For each one, pick up the rock and place it on the platform
  9. Once Paddy is up onto the top level, click the cave door to start the cave entrance minigame
  10. Around the egde you will see scribbles like 'P=1, A=5…' and a signing 'Paddy woz ere'. Use the keypad to enter the code for PADDY, which is 1,5,2,2,3
  11. Click Paddy to end the leve1
Hide

Level Five

Into the Monster's Lair

Show
  1. Click the crocodiles tail
  2. Click Paddy, he will jump to the land
  3. Click the crocodile again and it will swim away
  4. Click the monster
  5. Click the three worms to put them in the bucket
  6. On the right hand side there is a scrambled image, click the six panels to rotate them and create the correct image, the cave will shake and a horn will drop
  7. Click the horn to pick it up, then click the bats to scare them off
  8. Click the rope and then the fishing rod to attach the rope
  9. Click the bucket to add a worm to the fishing line
  10. When a fish is under the rope click the fishing rod to fish
  11. Once caught, click the fish to feed it to the monster
  12. Repeat Steps 8 – 11 once more
  13. Now click the monster, he will burp causing the rocks above to fall on him
  14. Click Paddy to end the level
Hide

Level Six

Diversion Ahead

Show
  1. Click the middle door, then click the bucket
  2. Click each rock in the hole in the road to put it in the bucket
  3. Click the middle cone and then the tar barrel to pick it up
  4. Click the bucket to pour the tar in
  5. Click the stick on the bus stop, then the bucket to stir the mixture
  6. Click the bucket and the mixture will be poured into the hole
  7. Click the two remaining cones to open the road
  8. Click the bus stop to open the route map
  9. Trace the route to the zoo...(read the next line if you want to know the route)
  10. The route you want is '35' so click the number 35
  11. Click Paddy and he will get on the bus
